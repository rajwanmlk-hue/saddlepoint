The Investment Case

Six Reasons to
Invest in Saudi Arabia

$3.3T Total Investment Pipeline
Unprecedented Capital Deployment
The Saudi government is deploying $3.3 trillion across infrastructure, tourism, tech, and real estate — creating demand far exceeding current supply in every major city.
0% Capital Gains Tax
One of the World's Most Competitive Tax Environments
No personal income tax. No capital gains tax on property. Transaction costs of just 2.5%. Saudi Arabia offers foreign investors exceptional after-tax returns unavailable in most Western markets.
100% Foreign Ownership Rights
Full Freehold Title Available
Since 2020 reforms, foreign nationals can own property outright in approved zones with no nationality restrictions, full inheritance rights, and long-term residency eligibility.
35M Population
Young, Growing Population Driving Demand
With 70% of Saudis under 35 and a rapidly growing urban middle class, residential and hospitality demand is structurally rising. Vision 2030's tourism targets add 100M+ visitors annually.
3.75 SAR/USD Fixed Rate
Dollar-Pegged Currency Stability
The Saudi Riyal has been pegged to the US Dollar since 1986. Investors face no currency volatility risk — a rare advantage for international capital deployment in emerging markets.
24h Title Transfer
World-Class Digital Infrastructure
Saudi Arabia's fully digital deed registration system enables title transfer within 24–48 hours. Transparent, efficient, and supported in English — among the fastest in the world.
Vision 2030

The Transformation
Blueprint

Vision 2030 is Saudi Arabia's national transformation plan — the most ambitious economic diversification programme in the Arab world. Every pillar creates direct real estate demand.

01 · Economic Diversification
Non-Oil GDP to 50%
Reducing oil dependence drives massive investment in finance, tourism, manufacturing, and entertainment — all requiring commercial and mixed-use real estate at scale.
Target: SAR 1.7T non-oil GDP by 2030
02 · Tourism & Entertainment
100 Million Visitors Annually
From virtually zero tourist visas in 2018 to a target of 100M annual visitors, hospitality demand across the Kingdom is undergoing structural transformation.
150+ new tourism destinations planned
03 · Urban Development
New Cities & Districts
NEOM, Red Sea Project, Qiddiya, and Diriyah Gate represent $1 trillion+ in entirely new urban environments, each requiring integrated real estate ecosystems from scratch.
$1T+ in mega-project real estate
04 · Population Growth
Urbanisation & Housing Demand
Vision 2030 targets 70% homeownership rates and is attracting skilled expatriate workers. Both drivers create sustained residential demand across all price points.
1.5M new homes needed by 2030

Vision 2030 is the primary driver of Saudi Arabia's real estate appreciation. The $3.3 trillion investment pipeline, mega-project development, and population growth targets create sustained demand pressure that historically translates to 12–42% capital appreciation across key markets.

Saudi Arabia offers one of the world's most competitive tax environments for property investors. A 15% VAT applies to commercial transactions, though exemptions are available in certain structures.
