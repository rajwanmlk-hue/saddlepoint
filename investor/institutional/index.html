Institutional Services

Portfolio Construction

End-to-end portfolio design matching your risk-adjusted return target — sector allocation, regional diversification, asset class weighting, and correlation analysis versus existing holdings.

Co-Investment Access

Exclusive participation in Vision 2030-aligned giga-project investments alongside PIF-linked entities — typically reserved for sovereign and institutional counterparties. Minimum co-invest: SAR 50M.

IC & Board Presentation Support

Full investment memoranda, financial models, sensitivity analyses and risk frameworks prepared to institutional standards. We support your IC or board process from first briefing to final approval.

IFRS-Standard Reporting

Quarterly portfolio reporting to IFRS and INREV standards — NAV updates, income statements, rent rolls, covenant compliance, and forward-looking scenario analysis. Audit-ready documentation.

REIT & SPV Structuring

Saudi REIT formation, SPV and holding company structuring across multiple jurisdictions, Islamic finance instrument advisory (Sukuk, Murabaha, Diminishing Musharakah) optimised for your LP base.

Exit Strategy Advisory

When the time comes, we run a disciplined exit process — secondary market sales, portfolio block transactions, and REIT conversion pathways — maximising residual value and minimising tax friction.
