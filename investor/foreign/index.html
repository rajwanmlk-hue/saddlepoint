Your Journey

From first call
to first title deed.

01

Discovery Call (60 min)

We understand your objectives, liquidity timeline, and risk appetite. No generic pitch decks — just a precise conversation about where your capital belongs in today's Saudi market.

02

Deal Selection & Due Diligence

We present 3–5 curated opportunities matching your mandate. Each comes with title verification, market comparables, legal status, and our independent valuation.

03

Legal Entity & SAGIA Registration

We coordinate your commercial registration through SAGIA. For eligible assets, we structure direct foreign ownership — no local partner, no revenue sharing. Timeline: 3 working days.

04

Transaction & Title Transfer

Our legal team manages the Sales and Purchase Agreement, funds transfer coordination, and title registration with the Ministry of Justice. You receive your title deed digitally.

05

Ongoing Asset Management & Reporting

Quarterly performance reports, rental income management, re-leasing, and exit strategy advisory. You stay fully informed without needing to be present in the Kingdom.

Documentation Required

What you'll need to begin.

Identity & Residency

  • Valid passport (all pages)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
  • National ID (where applicable)
  • Visa/residency information (for KSA entry)

Financial & Compliance

  • Source of funds declaration
  • Bank reference letter (6-month statements)
  • AML/KYC verification (we handle this)
  • Investment mandate letter (optional, for structures)

All documentation is handled through our secure client portal. Our compliance team manages SAGIA and RERA submissions on your behalf.
